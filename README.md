## Which Package Should You Actually Get?

Real talk — the **Journey 25/25 at R393/pm** is the sweet spot for most households. That's fast enough for 4K Netflix, video calls, and a few people using it at once. The Journey 10/10 at R293/pm works fine if you're solo and just browsing and streaming in HD.

If you work from home and spend half your day on Zoom or uploading large files, the **Journey 100/100 at R793/pm** makes sense. Symmetric upload and download speeds matter when you're the one sharing your screen on a call rather than just watching.

And if you can swing the upfront payment — **pay 11 months and get month 12 free.** On the Journey 25/25 that's basically saving you R393. That's a decent dinner out.

## The Open Access Network — What That Actually Means For You

Here's something most ISPs won't advertise: Accelerit's own fibre network is an **open access network.** That means if you're not happy with Accelerit as your ISP down the line, you can switch to another ISP on the same backbone without re-doing your installation or paying new setup fees.

That's a genuinely consumer-friendly setup. It means you're not just buying into the ISP — you're buying into the infrastructure, and you keep your options open.
